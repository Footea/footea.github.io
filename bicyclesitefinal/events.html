Join Us!

Come ride the Montana section of the Contential Divide Trail. This is a great trip. We like to do about 25 miles a day. You can ride at your own page and finish the trek in 1-4 days depending on your skill level. We offer stations throughout the trail as well that has service and firstaid and snacks and water. This once a year ride is a great place to practice learned skills and meet new friends and enjoy a great party at the end of the trail.
